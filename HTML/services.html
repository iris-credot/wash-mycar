OUR SERVICES

Mobile Car Wash.


Offer on-demand mobile car washing services where your team brings the necessary equipment and cleans cars at the customer's location, whether it's their home, office, or another convenient spot.



On-Demand Car Wash.


Allow customers to schedule car wash appointments online at a time and location that suits them. They can choose from different packages based on the level of cleaning they need.



Waterless Car Wash.


Provide an eco-friendly option by offering waterless car washing services that use specialized products to clean and shine the vehicle without the need for water.



Interior Detailing.


Offer thorough cleaning of the interior of the vehicle, including vacuuming, upholstery cleaning, dashboard polishing, and window cleaning.



Exterior Detailing.

Provide comprehensive exterior cleaning services, including hand washing, waxing, polishing, and tire cleaning.



Express Wash.


Offer a quick and efficient exterior cleaning service for customers who are looking for a basic wash and don't require extensive detailing.



Premium Detailing.


Provide high-end detailing services that include extensive interior and exterior cleaning, paint correction, and protection treatments like ceramic coating.



Fleet Services.


Cater to businesses with a fleet of vehicles by offering specialized pricing and services tailored to their needs.



Subscription Plans.


Offer subscription-based models where customers can pay a monthly fee for a certain number of car washes, encouraging repeat business.
